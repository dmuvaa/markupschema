About Us

A Space for Mindful Connection

Serenity Social Club is more than a community—it's a movement toward intentional living. We gather to share experiences that nourish the mind, body, and spirit.

From morning yoga sessions in lush gardens to thought-provoking book discussions, from creative workshops to meaningful conversations around the table—every gathering is designed to help you slow down and connect with what matters.
